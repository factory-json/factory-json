# Sunrise Textiles Pvt. Ltd.

Vertically integrated knit garment manufacturer specializing in organic cotton basics and activewear. Founded 1997. 500–600 employees. Languages: Tamil, English, Hindi.

## Capabilities

### Processes

Knitting, dyeing, cut-and-sew, embroidery, screen printing, sublimation printing, garment washing.

### Materials

Organic cotton, BCI cotton, cotton-polyester blends, viscose, modal, recycled polyester, Tencel/lyocell, bamboo viscose.

### Finishes

Enzyme wash, silicone softener, anti-pilling, moisture wicking, wrinkle-free, UV protection, anti-microbial.

### Fabric Types

Jersey, interlock, rib, pique, french terry, fleece. GSM range: 120–400.

### Garment Types

T-shirts, polo shirts, hoodies, joggers, leggings, tank tops. Size range: XS–5XL. Color matching: Pantone TPX/TCX.

### Secondary Services

Pattern making/grading, labeling and tagging, hang tags and packaging, poly bagging, carton packing, retail-ready packaging.

### Tolerances

- **Standard:** ±0.5 cm (body measurements per size spec)
- **By process:** cutting ±0.3cm, sewing (seam placement) ±0.2cm, print placement ±0.3cm, embroidery placement ±0.2cm
- **Min feature:** 5 mm embroidery detail

### Constraints

- **MOQ:** 500–50,000 pieces
- **Lead time:** 21–45 days (10 days for samples, expedite available)
- **Capacity:** 50,000 pieces/month
- **Min order value:** $2,000 USD

## Quality

AQL 2.5 final inspection, inline QC at cutting and sewing, pre-shipment lab test report. Dedicated QC team of 15 inspectors. Defect rate: 2,500 PPM.

### Testing

Fabric GSM testing, color fastness (wash, rub, light — ISO 105 series), shrinkage testing (AATCC 135), pilling resistance (ISO 12945), dimensional stability, needle detection, tensile/burst strength.

### Documentation

AQL inspection report, lab test report (color fastness, shrinkage, GSM), packing list, certificate of conformance (CoC), GOTS transaction certificate, OEKO-TEX test report.

### Traceability

GOTS-compliant chain of custody from fiber to finished garment. Each lot linked to organic cotton source and dyeing batch records.
